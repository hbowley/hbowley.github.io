End-to-end statistical analysis pipeline for child Eye Blink Conditioning research, part of the Project Seahorse study at FIU. Modeled trace eyeblink conditioning to assess individual and sex differences in learning and memory processes.

**Highlights:**
- Reproducible analysis workflow in R
- Complex repeated-measures and longitudinal designs
- Publication-quality figures with ggplot2
- Contributed to peer-reviewed publications and conference presentations

**Tech:** R, tidyverse, ggplot2, Statistical Modeling
