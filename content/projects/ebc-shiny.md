Built an interactive Shiny application that automates the scoring process for Eye Blink Conditioning (EBC) research tasks. What used to be a tedious manual process now runs in seconds with consistent, reproducible results.

**What it does:**
- Ingests raw EBC trial data
- Applies scoring algorithms automatically
- Generates visual summaries of conditioning patterns
- Exports clean, analysis-ready datasets

**Tech:** R, Shiny, Data Processing, Research Automation
