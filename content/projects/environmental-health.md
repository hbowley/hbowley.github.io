Conducted an environmental health analysis for the Florida Department of Health in partnership with the University of Miami Sylvester Comprehensive Cancer Center.

**What I did:**
- Analyzed lung cancer incidence trends across seven Florida counties
- Leveraged R and automated data extraction for efficient processing
- Developed a data-driven community mitigation plan
- Informed tobacco cessation efforts with actionable analytics

**Impact:** Findings directly informed public health interventions and tobacco cessation programming across multiple counties.

**Tech:** R, Automated Data Extraction, Public Health Analytics, Epidemiology
