## Short version

I am a data science leader and biostatistician based in Miami. I currently serve as Director of Research and Data Analytics at Miami Dade College and as Principal Data Scientist at Portable Networks. I build practical analytics and machine learning systems that help teams make better decisions.

## What I do

My work sits at the intersection of analytics leadership, applied machine learning, and production data products.

- Lead analytics strategy and execution across complex organizations
- Build and deploy decision support tools in R and Python
- Turn messy operational data into clear, useful workflows
- Coach technical teams and raise analytics standards

## Current focus

Right now I am focused on:

- Student success analytics and retention modeling
- Applied AI and automation for institutional workflows
- Scalable BI applications for client and stakeholder use
- Reproducible research and reporting systems

## Background

I started in research, where I worked on NIH funded projects in neurodevelopment and spatial cognition. That training shaped how I approach every problem today: ask better questions, use rigorous methods, and communicate findings clearly.

Over time, I moved from research execution into analytics leadership and consulting. I have led teams, built production Shiny and cloud based reporting tools, and supported high impact decisions across higher education and client environments.
